# What is Spatial Computing?

> 中文版本：[what-is-spatial-computing.md](what-is-spatial-computing.md)

---

## Definition

Spatial Computing is a computing paradigm that integrates digital content with physical space, allowing users to naturally interact with digital content in 3D space.

---

## Core Features

### 1. 3D Spatial Awareness
The system can understand and perceive real 3D space.

### 2. Natural Interaction
Supports natural interaction methods such as gestures, eye tracking, voice, etc.

### 3. Virtual-Real Fusion
Digital content can seamlessly integrate with the real world.
